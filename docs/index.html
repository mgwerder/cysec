What is Cysec?

Cysec is a open-source, extensible and flexible platform to coach users.

It's part of the SMESEC EU project

Who are the users?

This platform is convinced for:

  • Private persons
  • Associations
  • Companies
  • Governments
  • Non government organizations

Specific coaching

You can install diverse coaches you want to use. A coach is an intelligent survey, providing different questions to help you to improve your workflow.

If you want, you can develop your own coach. Its fully customizable and easy to use.

Constructive Feedback

Each coach gives you recommendations - depending on your answers - how you can improve your workflow.

Scores and a grades are calculated for each coach and informs you about your standing.

You can unlock badges as achievements by doing coaches.
